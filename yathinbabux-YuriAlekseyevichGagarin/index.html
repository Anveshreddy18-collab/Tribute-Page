Valentina Tereshkova

Here's a time line of Valentina's life:

  • 6 March 1937 - Born in Bolshoye Maslennikovo, Russia.
  • 16 February 1962 - Valentina Tereshkova was selected to join the female cosmonaut corps.
  • 16 June 1963 - Vostok 6 launched faultlessly, and Tereshkova became the first woman in space.
  • 1977 - She earned a doctorate in engineering.
  • 1966 - 1991 - Due to her prominence, Tereshkova was chosen for several political positions.
  • 1966 - She became a member of the World Peace Council.
  • 2011 - She was elected to the State Duma, the lower house of the Russian legislature, as a member of United Russia where she continues to serve.
